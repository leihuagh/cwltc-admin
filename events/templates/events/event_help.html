Help on tournament entry

Singles events

To enter a singles event, just press Enter event.

You can cancel your entry at any time with the Cancel event button.

Doubles events

To enter, first choose your partner with the Select partner button. Enter a few characters of your intended partner's first name, a space, then a few characters of their last name until their name appears in the list. Then click their name to select them. The Enter with partner button will then be visible.

Entering without a partner

If you have no agreed partner for a doubles tournament but would like to play, you can enter as a single. The tournament director will pair you up with another single if there is one available, before the draw.

Alternatively, if you see a single in the list of entrants with whom you would like to partner, cancel any single entry that you have already made, then select them as your partner and press Enter with partner. They will automatically be removed from the singles list and assigned as your partner.
