Club News

New online services

We have upgraded the internet service in the clubhouse, significantly increasing the speed and range of the wi-fi. There is now an iPad mounted on the wall to the right of the kitchen hatch that offers 3 new online services:
Tea and coffee sales

You can now charge teas, coffee and cakes to your club account in the same way as bar purchases. The old teas book is no longer necessary.

Visitors book

The paper-based visitors book is now obsolete. Adults and juniors 13 and over should now record their visitors online through the iPad.

Members database search

You can now search the database of adult members to find a phone number or email address. Only adults who agreed to share their details are shown.

You can also search the database through the Search menu on this website.

Members' Handbook

We have published a new handbook for members that details all the information that you need to get the best out of the club. It is particularly relevant to new members, but established members will also benefit from reading it. Parents of juniors should also read the separate document, Information for Juniors.
